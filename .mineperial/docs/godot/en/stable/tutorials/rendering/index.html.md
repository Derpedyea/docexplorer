**Rendering**  
*Godot Engine (stable) – Tutorial Index*  

This page serves as an entry point to the rendering‑related tutorials in the Godot Engine documentation.  
Below is a quick overview of the main topics you can explore from here:

- **Overview of renderers** – Learn about the available renderers in Godot (Forward+, Mobile, Compatibility) and how to choose the right one for your project.  
- **Using Viewports** – Master how to create and manipulate viewports to render scenes into textures, create split‑screens, or implement custom UI overlays.  
- **Multiple resolutions** – Discover techniques for handling different screen sizes and aspect ratios, and how to keep your graphics crisp on all devices.  
- **Fixing jitter, stutter and input lag** – A troubleshooting guide to keep your games smooth, with solutions for common frame‑rate and latency issues.  
- **The Compositor** – Explore the post‑processing pipeline, shaders, and visual effects that can be applied after the scene has been rendered.
